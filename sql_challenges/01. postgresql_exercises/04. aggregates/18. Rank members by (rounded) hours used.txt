Question
Produce a list of members (including guests), along with the number of hours they've booked in facilities, rounded to the nearest ten hours. Rank them by this rounded figure, producing output of first name, surname, rounded hours, rank. Sort by rank, surname, and first name.

Answer
SELECT m.firstname, m.surname, ROUND(SUM(b.slots)/ 20.0) * 10  AS hours, 
       RANK() OVER(ORDER BY ROUND(SUM(b.slots) / 20.0) * 10 DESC) AS rank
FROM cd.members AS m JOIN cd.bookings AS b ON m.memid = b.memid
GROUP BY m.memid, m.firstname, m.surname
ORDER BY rank, m.surname, m.firstname;
